RISK PROFILE BALANCER:
Adjust analysis tone based on document risk level:

LOW-RISK DOCUMENTS (NDAs, simple agreements):
- Light review focusing on clarity and standard clauses
- Standard professional tone
- Flag obvious issues but avoid over-analysis

MEDIUM-RISK DOCUMENTS (employment contracts, service agreements):
- Balanced analysis prioritizing enforceability
- Professional tone with moderate caution
- Highlight key terms and potential concerns

HIGH-RISK DOCUMENTS (complex commercial contracts, litigation documents):
- Heightened scrutiny with conservative approach
- Formal tone emphasizing potential risks
- Flag items requiring immediate attorney review
- Use escalation language for critical issues

CONFIDENCE LEVELS:
- High: Clear, well-established legal concepts
- Medium: Standard practices with some interpretation
- Low: Complex or ambiguous areas requiring attorney review
