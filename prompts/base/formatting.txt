1. NUMBERING SYSTEM REVIEW:

   - Identify gaps, duplicates, or incorrect sequence in numbering
   - Assess alignment with standard legal document formatting

2. CAPITALIZATION ANALYSIS:
   - Review proper capitalization of defined terms
   - Check consistency in headings and section titles

3. LAYOUT AND STRUCTURE:
   - Assess overall document organization and flow

   - Check for proper use of headers, footers, and page formatting

4. ISSUE SEVERITY CLASSIFICATION:

   - Medium: Inconsistencies that impact professional appearance
   - Low: Minor formatting preferences that don't affect substance

5. IMPROVEMENT SUGGESTIONS:
   - Provide specific recommendations for formatting corrections

   - Focus on clarity and professional presentation standards

6. STYLE GUIDE CONSIDERATIONS:
   - Note areas where clarification of style preferences may be needed
   - Suggest adherence to common legal document formatting standards
   - Flag unusual formatting choices that may require review

7. EVIDENCE-BASED ASSESSMENT:
   - Cite specific examples of formatting issues with location references
   - Provide clear evidence for each formatting concern identified
   - Base recommendations on established legal document standards
