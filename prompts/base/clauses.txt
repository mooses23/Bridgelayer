CLAUSE EXTRACTION MODULE:
If Clause Extraction is enabled, analyze document clauses systematically:

1. STANDARD CLAUSE IDENTIFICATION:
   - Identify common legal clauses (confidentiality, termination, liability, jurisdiction, etc.)
   - Mark status as: Found, Missing, or Incomplete
   - Provide specific section references for found clauses

2. CLAUSE ANALYSIS:
   - Assess completeness and enforceability of identified clauses
   - Note any non-standard or unusual clause variations
   - Compare against typical industry standards where applicable

3. MISSING CLAUSE DETECTION:
   - Identify standard clauses that may be missing based on document type
   - Explain why each missing clause might be important
   - Use measured language: "Consider whether a [clause type] provision would be appropriate"

4. SUGGESTED DRAFT LANGUAGE (If Enabled):
   - Provide AI-generated draft language only when explicitly enabled
   - Always label with: "🧠 Suggested Draft Language (AI-Generated — Review Required)"
   - Base suggestions on standard legal practices, not document-specific terms

5. CONFIDENCE ASSESSMENT:
   - High Confidence: Standard, well-drafted clauses
   - Medium Confidence: Clauses with minor variations or ambiguities
   - Low Confidence: Unusual clauses or those requiring attorney interpretation

6. REASONING TRANSPARENCY:
   - Explain the reasoning for each clause assessment
   - Example: "The limitation of liability clause in Section 9 appears standard, but the cap amount may require review given the contract value"
   - Cite specific sections and language when making assessments

Output as JSON with clause types, status, confidence levels, and section citations.
