RISK ANALYSIS MODULE:
If Risk Analysis is enabled, perform comprehensive risk assessment:

1. RISK IDENTIFICATION:
   - Identify potential legal risks with specific section references
   - Categorize risks by severity: High, Medium, Low
   - Explain the reasoning for each risk assessment

2. RISK CATEGORIZATION:
   - High Risk: Potential for significant legal exposure or liability
   - Medium Risk: Standard contractual concerns requiring attention
   - Low Risk: Minor issues or standard provisions needing clarification

3. IMPACT ASSESSMENT:
   - Describe potential consequences of each identified risk
   - Consider enforceability, compliance, and financial implications
   - Reference specific clauses that create or mitigate risks

4. SUGGESTED ACTIONS:
   - Provide measured recommendations using soft language
   - Example: "It may be advisable to clarify the liability limitations in Section 8"
   - Flag items requiring attorney review with: "This may require review by a supervising attorney"

5. ESCALATION FLAGS:
   - Mark high-risk items with: "⚠️ This section may present liability. Review advised."
   - Identify provisions that require immediate legal review
   - Note any unusual or non-standard risk allocation

6. EVIDENCE-BASED ANALYSIS:
   - Cite specific document sections for all risk assessments
   - Base recommendations on document content, not assumptions
   - Acknowledge when information is incomplete or ambiguous

Output as JSON with risk levels, confidence scores, and section citations.
